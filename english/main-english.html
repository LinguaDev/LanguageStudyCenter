English Levels

A1 - Beginner

Start your English journey here! Learn basic vocabulary, simple sentences, and understand everyday expressions.

A2 - Elementary

Build upon your basics. Understand common English phrases, describe your personal background, and have simple conversations.

B1 - Intermediate

Reach intermediate fluency. Understand main points in clear standard English, navigate most travel situations, and confidently express your opinions.

B2 - Upper-Intermediate

Refine your English skills. Understand complex texts, interact fluently in English, and produce detailed writing on a wide array of subjects.

C1 - Advanced

Achieve advanced English proficiency. Understand a wide range of demanding, longer texts, and express yourself fluently and spontaneously in English.

C2 - Proficiency

Master English at the proficiency level. Easily understand virtually everything, summarize information from various sources, and express yourself with nuanced fluency.

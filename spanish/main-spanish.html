Spanish Levels

A1 - Beginner

Basic vocabulary, simple sentences, understanding everyday expressions.

A2 - Elementary

B1 - Intermedio

Understanding main points of clear standard input, dealing with most travel situations, expressing opinions.

B2 - Upper-Intermediate

Understanding complex texts, interacting fluently, detailed text on a wide range of subjects.

C1 - Advanced

Understanding a wide range of demanding, longer texts, expressing oneself fluently and spontaneously.

C2 - Proficiency

Easily understanding virtually everything heard or read, summarizing information, expressing oneself very fluently.

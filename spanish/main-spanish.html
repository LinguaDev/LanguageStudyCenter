Spanish Levels

A1 - Beginner

Basic vocabulary, simple sentences, understand everyday expressions. Perfect for starting your Spanish journey.

A2 - Elementary

Understand common phrases, describe your personal background, and engage in simple conversations in Spanish.

B1 - Intermediate

Understand the main points of clear standard Spanish, handle travel situations, and express your opinions effectively.

B2 - Upper-Intermediate

Understand complex Spanish texts, interact fluently in conversations, and write detailed text on various subjects.

C1 - Advanced

Understand a wide range of demanding, longer Spanish texts and express yourself fluently and spontaneously in most situations.

C2 - Proficiency

Easily understand virtually everything heard or read in Spanish, summarize information, and express yourself very fluently in nuanced ways.

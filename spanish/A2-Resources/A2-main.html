What You'll Learn in the A2 Spanish Course

Building on your A1 knowledge, the A2 Spanish course will enhance your ability to communicate in everyday situations with greater confidence and complexity. Explore new topics and grammatical structures to expand your Spanish proficiency.

  • Talking About Your Daily Routine: Describe your daily activities and habits in Spanish, expanding your vocabulary and verb usage.
  • Describing Your Home and Surroundings: Learn to describe your living space and immediate environment in detail.
  • Expressing Likes and Dislikes: Communicate your preferences and aversions effectively in Spanish.
  • Making Simple Requests and Offers: Politely ask for things and make offers using appropriate Spanish phrases.
  • Understanding and Using Basic Past Tenses: Begin to navigate the past tense, enabling you to talk about past events and experiences.
  • Ordering Food in a Restaurant: Master ordering meals and interacting in restaurant settings with more specific vocabulary.
  • Asking For and Giving Directions: Improve your wayfinding skills by learning more complex direction-giving and -receiving vocabulary and phrases.
  • Expanding Everyday Vocabulary: Broaden your Spanish vocabulary across a range of common, everyday topics and scenarios.

This A2 course is designed to smoothly transition you from beginner to elementary Spanish speaker. Through engaging lessons and practical exercises, you’ll improve your fluency, accuracy, and overall confidence in using Spanish in a variety of contexts. Get ready to handle more complex conversations and start understanding simple written Spanish!

Course Features:

  • Interactive Video Lessons with Native Speakers: Learn from engaging video lessons featuring native Spanish speakers, enhancing your listening and comprehension skills.
  • Engaging Exercises and Quizzes with Instant Feedback: Solidify your understanding with interactive exercises and quizzes that provide immediate feedback for effective learning.
  • Downloadable Study Materials for Offline Learning: Access and download comprehensive study materials, allowing you to reinforce your learning anytime, anywhere, even offline.
  • Access to a Supportive Online Community (Optional): Join our supportive online community to connect with fellow learners, practice your Spanish, and receive encouragement.
  • Regular Progress Checks and Personalized Recommendations: Monitor your progress with regular checks and receive personalized recommendations to guide your learning journey effectively.
  • Practical Exercises for Real-Life Scenarios: Apply your Spanish skills in practical exercises designed to simulate real-life conversations and situations, enhancing your practical usage.

What You Will Master in the C1 Spanish Course

Welcome to the C1 Advanced Spanish course, designed to bring your Spanish skills to a near-native level. This rigorous course will equip you with the linguistic tools needed to understand and express yourself in complex, nuanced, and sophisticated Spanish across diverse contexts.

  • Understanding a Wide Range of Demanding, Longer Texts: Develop expert reading skills to comprehend complex and lengthy texts, including literary works, academic articles, and specialized professional documents.
  • Expressing Yourself Fluently and Spontaneously: Achieve near-native fluency, enabling you to express yourself effortlessly and spontaneously in spoken Spanish, with minimal hesitation.
  • Using Language Flexibly and Effectively: Master the art of using Spanish flexibly and effectively for social, academic, and professional purposes, adapting your style and register to suit any situation.
  • Producing Clear, Well-Structured, Detailed Text on Complex Subjects: Enhance your writing proficiency to produce coherent, well-organized, and detailed written pieces on complex and abstract topics.
  • Understanding Implicit Meaning and Nuances in Communication: Develop advanced listening skills to discern implicit meanings, cultural nuances, and subtle undertones in both spoken and written communication.
  • Engaging in Complex and Nuanced Discussions: Confidently participate in sophisticated and nuanced discussions on a wide array of topics, demonstrating critical thinking and advanced linguistic command.
  • Expressing Ideas with Precision and Sophistication: Refine your expressive abilities to convey intricate ideas with precision, sophistication, and stylistic accuracy in Spanish.

The C1 Advanced Spanish course represents a significant step towards full Spanish proficiency. Building upon your robust B2 foundation, this course delves into the intricacies of the Spanish language, challenging you to achieve a command that is both broad and deep. Through expert-led instruction and challenging materials, you will refine your linguistic accuracy, expand your idiomatic range, and gain the confidence to navigate any communicative challenge in Spanish. Prepare to communicate with mastery and sophistication, unlocking unparalleled opportunities in your personal, academic, and professional life.

Course Features:

  • Advanced Video Lectures and Discussions with Experts: Learn from engaging, high-level video lectures and in-depth discussions led by leading experts in Spanish linguistics and culture, designed to challenge and inspire.
  • In-depth Analysis of Authentic Materials (Literature, Film, News): Deepen your understanding of Spanish through critical analysis of a rich selection of authentic materials, including classic and contemporary literature, acclaimed films, and current news media.
  • Personalized Feedback on Written and Oral Assignments: Receive detailed, personalized feedback on all written and oral assignments from experienced instructors, tailored to help you overcome specific challenges and refine your advanced skills.
  • Access to a Professional Network of Spanish Speakers (Optional): Expand your professional horizons by gaining access to a network of fluent and native Spanish speakers, offering opportunities for collaboration, networking, and real-world language practice.
  • Opportunities for Independent Research and Presentation: Develop your academic and professional skills through opportunities for independent research projects and presentations in Spanish, enhancing your ability to work autonomously and present complex information.
  • Dedicated Mentorship from Experienced Language Instructors: Benefit from one-on-one mentorship from our most experienced language instructors, providing personalized guidance, support, and strategies for achieving C1 mastery and beyond.

Welcome to Lesson 19: Hobbies and Free Time Activities! Expanding your vocabulary is key to fluency, and what better way to do it than by learning to talk about fun things like **hobbies and free time activities**! In this lesson, you will learn essential Spanish words and phrases to discuss what you and others enjoy doing in your leisure time. Knowing how to talk about hobbies is not only practical but also a great way to connect with Spanish speakers and share personal interests. We will cover a wide range of activities, providing examples and practice to help you confidently talk about your hobbies in Spanish. Let's dive into the world of Spanish hobbies!

Welcome to Lesson 11: Asking for Directions - Basic Phrases! In this lesson, you will learn essential Spanish phrases to confidently ask for directions. Being able to ask for directions is crucial for navigating in Spanish-speaking countries and getting around with ease. We will cover phrases for starting the conversation, asking where places are, and understanding responses. Let's begin learning how to ask for "direcciones" in Spanish!

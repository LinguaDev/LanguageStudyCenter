Welcome to Lesson 23: Expressing Likes and Dislikes! Being able to express what you like and dislike is fundamental to expressing your personality and engaging in conversations. In this lesson, you will learn essential **Spanish phrases for expressing likes and dislikes**. Whether you're talking about food, hobbies, activities, or opinions, these phrases will be incredibly useful. We will cover a range of expressions, from simple preferences to stronger likes and dislikes, providing examples and practice to help you confidently communicate your tastes and aversions in Spanish. Let's get started and prepare you to express yourself clearly in Spanish!

Welcome to Lesson 22: Clothes and Shopping! Talking about clothes and shopping is not only practical but also a fun way to engage with Spanish culture! In this lesson, you will learn essential **Spanish vocabulary and phrases for clothes and shopping**. Whether you're planning a shopping trip, describing your outfit, or just browsing in a store, this vocabulary will be incredibly useful. We will cover a wide range of clothing items, from everyday wear to special occasion outfits, as well as phrases for navigating shopping situations. Let's get started and prepare you to talk about fashion and shopping in Spanish!

Welcome to Lesson 3: Spanish Numbers! In this expanded lesson, you will learn to count in Spanish, mastering numbers not just to twenty, but understanding the system to count into the thousands and millions. Numbers are fundamental in daily life, and this lesson will equip you with the knowledge to use them confidently. We will cover number names, structural patterns, and practice applying them in various contexts. Let's start mastering Spanish numbers!

Welcome to Lesson 12: Places in a City! In this lesson, you will learn essential Spanish vocabulary for common places you find in a city. Knowing these words is vital for giving and understanding directions, exploring urban environments, and general conversations about city life. We will cover a variety of places, from shops and restaurants to parks and public buildings. Let's get started and explore "los lugares de la ciudad" in Spanish!

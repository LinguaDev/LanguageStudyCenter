Welcome to Lesson 21: Talking about the Weather! Weather is a universal conversation starter! In this lesson, you will learn essential **Spanish vocabulary and phrases for talking about the weather**. Whether you're planning a trip, making small talk, or just curious about today's conditions, knowing weather vocabulary is incredibly useful. We will cover a variety of weather terms, from sunny days to stormy nights, providing examples and practice to help you confidently discuss the weather in Spanish. Let's get started and prepare you to chat about the climate in Spanish!

Welcome to Lesson 18: Using "Ser" and "Estar" (Basics)! One of the most distinctive features of Spanish grammar is having **two verbs for "to be": "Ser" and "Estar."** This lesson will introduce you to the fundamental differences between these verbs and their basic uses. Understanding when to use "Ser" versus "Estar" is crucial for speaking correct and natural Spanish. We will focus on the core distinctions and provide clear examples and practice to help you grasp this essential concept. Let's unravel the mystery of "Ser" and "Estar"!

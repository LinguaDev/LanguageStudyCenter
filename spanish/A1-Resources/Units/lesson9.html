Welcome to Lesson 9: Ordering in a Cafe! In this lesson, you will learn how to confidently order food and drinks in a Spanish-speaking cafe or restaurant. This practical lesson will equip you with the phrases and vocabulary you need for a smooth and enjoyable cafe experience. We will cover everything from greetings and ordering to asking for the bill. Let's get started and learn how to order "en un café" in Spanish!

Welcome to Lesson 10: Common Drinks! In this lesson, you will learn essential Spanish vocabulary for common drinks. Knowing these words is crucial for social situations, ordering in cafes or restaurants, and understanding daily conversations about beverages. We will cover a variety of drinks, from basic water and juices to popular Hispanic drinks. Let's dive in and explore "las bebidas" in Spanish!

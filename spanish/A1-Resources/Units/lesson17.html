Welcome to Lesson 17: Common Adjectives! Now that you have a solid foundation in present tense verbs, we will expand your descriptive abilities by learning about **adjectives**! In this lesson, you will be introduced to essential Spanish adjectives that will help you describe people, places, things, and ideas more vividly. Adjectives are crucial for making your Spanish richer and more expressive. We will focus on common and useful adjectives and provide plenty of examples and practice to help you start using them right away. Let's begin expanding your Spanish vocabulary with adjectives!

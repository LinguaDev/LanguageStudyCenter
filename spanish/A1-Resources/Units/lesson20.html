Welcome to Lesson 20: Describing Your Daily Routine! Being able to talk about your day is essential for everyday communication. In this lesson, you will learn key **Spanish vocabulary and phrases to describe your daily routine**, from waking up in the morning to going to bed at night. This vocabulary will empower you to share information about your typical day, make plans, and understand others when they talk about their routines. We will cover a wide range of daily activities, providing examples and practice to ensure you can confidently describe your day in Spanish. Let's start building your vocabulary for daily life in Spanish!

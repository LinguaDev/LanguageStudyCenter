What You'll Learn in the A1 Spanish Course

In this beginner-friendly A1 course, you will gain foundational skills to start communicating in Spanish. Get ready to explore basic conversations and everyday situations!

  • Basic Greetings and Introductions: Learn to say "Hola," "Buenos d√≠as," and introduce yourself and others.
  • Numbers, Dates, and Telling Time: Master counting, discuss dates, and tell time in Spanish.
  • Talking about Family and Friends: Describe your family members and friends using simple Spanish phrases.
  • Ordering Food and Drinks: Learn essential phrases for ordering meals and beverages in restaurants and cafes.
  • Asking for Directions: Get around with ease by learning how to ask for and understand basic directions.
  • Simple Present Tense Verbs: Grasp the fundamentals of present tense verbs to form basic sentences.
  • Essential Vocabulary: Build a core vocabulary for everyday situations, including common objects, places, and actions.

This course is perfectly tailored for complete beginners with no prior Spanish knowledge. We provide a supportive and step-by-step learning experience to build a strong foundation for your Spanish language journey. Get ready to speak Spanish from day one and unlock a world of new communication!

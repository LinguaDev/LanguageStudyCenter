About LLC and a bit about Me!

Welcome to the Language Learning Center! I'm Juan Pineda, a Nicaraguan English and Spanish teacher, and I'm thrilled to share this project with you. All the resources on this site are completely free to use. I'm currently developing this platform as a way to practice and improve my web development skills, and I'm hosting it on GitHub.

My goal is to make language learning accessible to everyone. I believe that language opens doors to new opportunities and connections, and I'm passionate about helping others achieve their language learning goals. I hope you find the materials here useful!
